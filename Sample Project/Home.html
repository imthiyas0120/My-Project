Welcome!

KMI College of Engineering & Technology is one of the premier institutions in South Tamil Nadu situated Dindigul. The institution was established in 2008 by Titan Educational Trust with the exalted aim of uplifting rural students to excel in the field of Engineering and Technology. It is approved by AICTE and affiliated with Anna University, Chennai. The institution was Accredited by NAAC A Grade with 3.25 CGPA in 2021 and received Autonomous status in 2023.
